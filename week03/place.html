About South Africa

South Africa's landscape is a diverse, dramatic mix of a high, central plateau (Highveld/Bushveld) rimmed by the Great Escarpment, including the jagged Drakensberg mountains. It transitions from arid deserts in the west and Karoo to lush coastal wetlands in the east, featuring savannahs, forests, and the Cape Fold Belt.
