Healthy Living Website - Site Plan

Site Name

Healthy Living Hub – A resource for balanced nutrition, fitness, and wellness tips. I chose this name because it clearly communicates the purpose and is easy to remember.

Site Purpose

The site provides practical health resources, including meal plans, fitness routines, and mental wellness guides. It will also feature a newsletter form for updates and a trip/activity section for outdoor wellness ideas.

Scenarios

  • “What are some quick, healthy meals I can prepare during a busy week?”
  • “Where can I find beginner-friendly workout routines?”

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E8B57 (Sea Green) – used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: #F5F5DC (Beige) – used for background and body text areas.

Typography

Headings: Montserrat – clean and modern for emphasis.

Body Text: Open Sans – easy to read for longer content.
